About Our Library

Empowering the people of Jigawa State through knowledge, cultural Heritage and information access, since 1991

Preserving Knowledge, Empowering Minds to Inculcating reading culture to our Clients

The Jigawa State Library Board was established to provide comprehensive library and information services to the residents of Jigawa State and it's Divisional Libraries. In Gumel Divisional Library, Kazure, Ringim, Hadejia, Birninkudu, Birniwa, Kirikasamma, Kafin Hausa, Jahun, Babura, Roni,Taura, Garki, Gwaran Sabuwa, Gwaram Tsohuwa. The vision of the Board is to promote and enhance Educational and Research pursuits, Cultural, and Literacy heritage and inculcate Reading habit. is to be the leading provider of information resources and services that support education, research, and lifelong learning.

With our new digital transformation initiative, we've expanded our services beyond physical walls to provide 24/7 access to e-resources for students, researchers, and the general public across Jigawa State and beyond.

Our library automation system allows members to search our catalog online and offline, reserve books, check account status, and access digital resources from anywhere with an internet connection.
